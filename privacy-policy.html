Privacy Policy

At DealBuddy, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us.

What Data We Collect

We collect your Facebook public profile (name and ID) to help you schedule deal posts. We do not collect passwords or sensitive information.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for deal scheduling, tracking, and analytics within the app. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data, you can:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings.
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find "DealBuddy" and remove it.
  4. Or email us at: support@dealbuddy.com

For any concerns, please contact us.
