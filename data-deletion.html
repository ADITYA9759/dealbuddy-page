How to Delete Your Data

If you want to delete your account data stored by DealBuddy, please follow the steps below:

  1. Go to your Facebook account Settings.
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find "DealBuddy" in the list.
  4. Click "Remove" and confirm.
  5. This will delete all associated permissions and data from our system.

Alternatively, you can request manual deletion by contacting us at: support@yourdomain.com
